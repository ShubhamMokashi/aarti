FAQ

Questions we hear most often

An AMFI Registered Mutual Fund Distributor (MFD) is a professional licensed by AMFI to distribute mutual fund products. We are NOT investment advisers. We help investors select and purchase suitable mutual fund schemes and are compensated through AMC commissions on regular plans. Our ARN number (ARN-123456) can be verified on the AMFI website.
You can start a SIP with as little as ₹500 per month in most equity mutual funds. Many liquid and debt funds also have low minimums. Lumpsum investments typically start from ₹1,000 to ₹5,000 depending on the scheme. We help you identify the best starting point based on your monthly cash flow.
No. Mutual fund returns are not guaranteed and are subject to market risks. Returns shown in calculators on this website are illustrative estimates based on assumed rates. Actual returns may be higher or lower. No one — including ArthSaarthi — can guarantee specific returns from mutual fund investments.
Regular plans include a distributor commission paid by the AMC, resulting in a slightly higher expense ratio. Direct plans have no distributor commission. While direct plans may appear cheaper, working with a qualified distributor adds value through personalised advice, goal mapping, portfolio reviews, and ongoing support.
