Our Story

Born From a Love of
Earth & Style

Falls & Hangs was founded in 2016 by Meera Nair — a textile designer and sustainability advocate who believed the fashion industry could do better. Rooted in the weaving traditions of South India and inspired by global western silhouettes, the brand was built to bridge the gap between ethical manufacturing and contemporary style.

The Beginning

A Small Studio in
Chennai

What started as a 10-artisan studio in Chennai's textile district has grown into a 200+ craftsperson facility that supplies sustainable western wear to boutiques and brands across India, Europe, the Middle East, and Australia.

Today, Falls & Hangs is not just a clothing brand — it is a manufacturing partner, a design house, and a community of women who believe in dressing with purpose. Every collection is designed in-house, produced ethically, and shipped with love.

Our name itself captures our design language: the natural falls of fluid fabric, and the hangs of perfectly structured silhouettes — the two poles between which all great women's western wear lives.

Our Commitment

Fashion That Heals the Planet

Sustainability is not a feature for us — it is our foundation. Every decision we make considers the earth, the wearer, and the community.

Our Pillars

How We Make Fashion
Responsible

🌿
Organic Natural Fibres

We use only GOTS-certified organic cotton, pure linen, bamboo, and TENCEL™ lyocell — fibres grown without harmful pesticides or synthetic fertilisers. They're better for the soil, the farmer, and the wearer's skin.

💧
Water-Conscious Production

Our dyeing facility uses closed-loop water systems that recycle and treat process water. We use natural and low-impact dyes, reducing water consumption by up to 80% compared to conventional fashion manufacturing.

♻️
Zero-Waste Cutting

Our pattern-making team uses zero-waste and low-waste cutting techniques. All remaining fabric scraps are donated to local artisan groups or repurposed into accessories, reducing our material waste to under 3%.

📦
Plastic-Free Packaging

Our packaging is 100% plastic-free — every garment is wrapped in recycled tissue paper, placed in FSC-certified kraft boxes, and sealed with paper tape. Everything is home-compostable or recyclable.

Good For Your Body

Why Natural Fabrics Are Better For Your Health

Conventional fashion often uses toxic finishes and synthetic fibres that irritate skin, disrupt hormones, and carry chemical residues. Our fabrics are different — they are kind to your body from the inside out.

🌬️
Breathable & Thermoregulating

Organic cotton and linen allow skin to breathe naturally, regulating body temperature and preventing sweat buildup that leads to skin irritation.

🚫
No Harmful Chemicals

Our fabrics are free from azo dyes, formaldehyde finishes, and PFAS — chemicals commonly found in fast fashion that can be absorbed through the skin.

🧴
Hypoallergenic

Natural fibres are gentle on sensitive skin. Our organic cotton is certified safe for people with skin conditions like eczema and contact dermatitis.

🦠
Naturally Antimicrobial

Bamboo and linen have natural antimicrobial properties that reduce odour without synthetic chemical treatments — keeping you fresh all day.
